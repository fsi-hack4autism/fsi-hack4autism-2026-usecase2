Parent Input

AI Insights — Page 9
🚩 Vague language
"as appropriate to his level" — this phrase replaces the specific 62 wpm criterion that was in the 2023 and 2024 goals. Vague criteria are not measurable and may make this goal legally unenforceable.
Ask: "What numeric criterion will you use to measure mastery?"
IEP 2023 p.8 · IEP 2024 p.8
🚩 Service reduction
Speech reduced from 60 → 30 min/wk and converted to group setting. No mastery of prior speech goals is documented in the draft.
Ask: "Which speech goals from 2024 were mastered to justify this reduction? Show the data."
IEP 2024 p.12 · SLP progress notes
💡 Jargon decoded
LRE — Least Restrictive Environment: federal IDEA principle requiring Max to be educated alongside non-disabled peers to the maximum extent appropriate. 87% is high; verify pull-out timing doesn't strip him of preferred subjects.
⚠ Concern not addressed
"Team will continue to monitor" appears in 3 consecutive IEPs for the same recess concern. This phrase has no measurable obligation attached.
Ask: "Can we convert monitoring into a measurable social engagement goal with quarterly data review?"

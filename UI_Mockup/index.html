Screen 4 · Meeting script

A scrollable script parents can use during the IEP meeting.

Questions to Ask

Is the reduction from 1,800 to 360 minutes/week of direct special education instruction a typo or an intentional proposal?
Why was direct OT removed and the Praxis/Motor Planning goal area eliminated?
How does the team justify reducing direct speech when boundary and social-pragmatic needs remain safety concerns?
Can Page 6 be corrected so Neurological Impairment matches the eligibility narrative on Page 7?

Red Flags

Direct instruction drops by 24 hours per week.

Ask the team to restore 1,800 minutes/week unless they can show clear educational data supporting the reduction.

Safety protocols expanded without matching clinical support.

Request a social-emotional or behavioral goal and ask how boundary-intervention data will be integrated.

Evidence

Prior service grid.

2024 plan lists Functional Life Skills/Vocational instruction at 1,800 min/week, direct speech at 60 min/week, and direct OT at 30 min/week.

Parent opening statement.

“We appreciate the team’s work and want to understand the data behind these reductions so AK’s safety, independence, communication, and motor-planning needs remain fully supported.”
